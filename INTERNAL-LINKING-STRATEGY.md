## Contextual Link Placement

### Best Practices:
1. **First Mention Rule** - Link the first mention of a topic per page
2. **Relevant Context** - Only link when the anchor text is contextually relevant
3. **Maximum 3-5 Links** - Don't over-link within service pages
4. **Varied Anchor Text** - Use different anchor texts to avoid keyword stuffing
5. **"Learn More" Links** - Use in feature boxes and benefit sections
6. **CTA Links** - Place CTAs strategically before the user scrolls too far

### Linking Density:
- Service pages: 3-5 internal links per page
- Blog posts: 4-6 internal links per post
- Homepage: 8-12 internal links (already has multiple service cards)
